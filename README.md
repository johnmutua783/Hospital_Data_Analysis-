# Healthcare Data Analysis & Patient Insights

## Objective

The goal of this project was to analyze hospital patient data to uncover insights related to medical conditions, treatment costs, patient outcomes, and overall hospital performance.

---

## Dataset

The dataset contains patient-level hospital records including:

* Age and gender
* Medical condition
* Procedure performed
* Treatment cost
* Length of hospital stay
* Readmission status
* Patient satisfaction score

---

## Tools Used

* Python (Pandas)
* Matplotlib

---

## Data Exploration

The dataset was explored to understand structure, data types, and statistical distributions using Pandas.

---

## Exploratory Data Analysis

The analysis focused on answering key healthcare questions:

* What are the most common medical conditions?
* What is the average treatment cost?
* Which conditions are most expensive?
* Which conditions require longer hospital stays?
* What is the readmission rate?
* How satisfied are patients overall?

---

## Key Insights

* The average patient age is approximately **54 years**, indicating a middle-aged patient population.

* **Cancer and heart-related conditions** are the most expensive to treat, with significantly higher costs compared to other conditions.

* Conditions such as **Cancer, Heart Attack, and Stroke** require longer hospital stays, indicating higher severity.

* The majority of patients were **not readmitted**, suggesting generally effective treatments, though some conditions show higher readmission rates.

* The average patient satisfaction score is **moderate (around 3.6/5)**, indicating room for improvement in patient experience.

* Satisfaction varies significantly by condition, with some treatments receiving much lower ratings.

---

## Recommendations

* Hospitals should focus on **cost management strategies** for high-expense conditions like cancer and heart diseases.

* Improve care processes for conditions with **low satisfaction scores** to enhance patient experience.

* Monitor conditions with higher **readmission rates** to improve treatment effectiveness.

* Allocate more resources to conditions requiring **longer hospital stays** for better patient outcomes.

---

## Conclusion

This project demonstrates how healthcare data can be analyzed using Python to uncover trends in cost, treatment outcomes, and patient satisfaction. The insights can support better hospital management and improved patient care strategies.
